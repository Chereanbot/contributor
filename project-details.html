Dilla University Legal Aid Service Management System

A comprehensive web-based solution for efficient legal aid case management

Project Objectives

General Objective

The main objective of this project is to design a web-based Legal Aid Case Management System (LACMS) for Dilla University.

Specific Objectives

  • Develop a digital platform for client case registration
  • Replace manual processes with efficient digital systems
  • Design user-friendly interfaces for all skill levels
  • Streamline case assignment and notification system
  • Implement secure data management and storage
  • Enable remote access to services
